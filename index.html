Co-founder/Former President of St. Louis Teens Aid Refugees Today

Co-founder and President of START (St. Louis Teens Aid Refugees Today), a teen-led organization that xwseeks to aid refugees in their transition to life in St. Louis. START is still running today and has partnered with another organization, Welcome Neighbor STL, to better assist members of the community. Retrieval Augmented Generation Chatbot for Boardgame Q&A
Jan 2024 - May 2024

As my senior capstone, worked in a team of 6 to create the Arbiter, a RAG Chatbot that specializes in answering ruling questions pertaining to board games. The Arbiter parses uploaded board game manuals and the BoardGameGeek.com forums to accurately retrieve context for questions. To minimize hallucination, the Arbiter does not answer if it's at a confidence level of less than 60%.
DraftRuneterra.com - Drafting Site for Riot Games' Legends of Runeterra
Summer 2022 - Winter 2023

As a personal project, lead the development of DraftRuneterra.com, a webapp that allows fans of Riot Games' Legends of Runeterra to draft cards in order to make a deck that is playable in the game. The website was built from the ground up and involved making a custom server that manages sessions for all logged in users, syncing with Riot Games' Developer API, and using React.js to build a front end from scratch.
